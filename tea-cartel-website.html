Our Story

Where every cup starts a conversation.

At Tea Cartel, we believe great conversations begin with a great cup of tea. What started as a simple idea — to create a warm, welcoming space for people to connect, relax, and enjoy quality food — has grown into one of Jaipur's favourite hangout spots.

Whether you're meeting friends, catching up on work, or simply taking a break from a busy day, Tea Cartel offers the perfect blend of comfort, flavour, and atmosphere. More than just a café — it's where memories are made.

FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Where is Tea Cartel located?
+
We're located in Jagatpura, Jaipur, Rajasthan. You can get directions via Google Maps or call us at +91 95303 83938 for help finding us.
What are your opening hours?
+
We're open every day from 10:00 AM to 12:00 AM (midnight), including weekends and public holidays. Come for chai in the morning or wind down with us in the evening.
Do you offer home delivery?
+
Yes! You can order from us on Zomato and Swiggy for home delivery. Just search "Tea Cartel Jagatpura" or use the Order Online section on this page.
Is the seating open-air? Is it suitable for evenings?
+
Yes! Tea Cartel has a beautiful open-air ambience that's especially lovely in the evenings. The relaxed outdoor setting is perfect for conversations, casual hangouts, and golden-hour moments.
Do you serve non-vegetarian food?
+
Our current menu is entirely vegetarian, featuring a wide range of teas, cold coffees, burgers, momos, Chinese dishes, and soups — all made fresh. We're proud to cater to every palate.
Can I reserve a table for a group or special occasion?
+
Absolutely! Call us at +91 95303 83938 or drop us a message on Instagram @_tea_cartel to arrange for a group visit or celebration. We love making memories happen.
What's your most popular item?
+
Without a doubt, our Cold Coffee and Kulhad Chai are the crowd favourites! The Tandoori Momos and Special Burger also have a massive fanbase. We recommend trying them all!
